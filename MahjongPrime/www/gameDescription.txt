Mahjong Prime - Solitaire Puzzle

Short Description:
Classic Mahjong Solitaire with 6 layouts, 3 themes, and offline play!

Full Description:
Mahjong Prime is a beautifully crafted, free, and open source Mahjong Solitaire game. Match open pairs of identical tiles and remove them from the board to reveal the tiles beneath. Clear all pairs to win!

Designed for both phones and tablets with HD graphics that look stunning on any screen size.

FEATURES:

6 Unique Layouts:
- Turtle - The timeless classic layout
- Flower - An elegant floral arrangement
- Spider - A challenging web of tiles
- Cloud - A flowing, airy design
- Bug - A fun insect-shaped puzzle
- Four Hills - A scenic mountain formation

3 Beautiful Themes:
- Classic - Traditional Mahjong tile designs
- Fruits - Colorful fruit-themed tiles
- High Visibility - Bold, easy-to-read tiles for accessibility

9 Languages:
English, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Turkish, and Russian

Gameplay Features:
- Hints to help you find matching pairs
- Undo moves when you make a mistake
- Smooth animations and transitions
- Game statistics and achievements tracking
- Timer and move counter

Privacy & Respect:
- Works completely offline - no internet required
- No ads - ever
- No tracking or analytics
- No in-app purchases
- No special permissions required
- All game data stored locally on your device only
