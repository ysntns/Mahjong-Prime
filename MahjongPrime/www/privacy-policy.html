Privacy Policy

Mahjong Prime by CerebrAI-VorTX (Yasin Tanis)

Effective Date: March 12, 2026 | Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Summary: Mahjong Prime is an offline solitaire Mahjong game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data whatsoever.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mahjong Prime ("the App"), developed by Yasin Tanis and CerebrAI-VorTX ("we", "us", or "our"), handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Mahjong Prime is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect any personal information or user data. Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected (no name, email, phone number, or address)
  • No health data is collected, accessed, or processed
  • No location data is collected
  • No device identifiers are collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking is implemented
  • No cookies or similar technologies are used
  • No advertisements are displayed
  • No third-party SDKs are integrated

3. Game Data Storage

The App stores minimal game-related data locally on your device only using browser localStorage or the app sandbox:

  • Game progress: Current game state and scores
  • Preferences: Your selected theme, layout, and language settings
  • Statistics: Game win/loss records and play history

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any external server. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the App.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Since we do not collect any user data, we do not share any data with third parties. The App:

  • Does not integrate any third-party analytics services
  • Does not include any advertising SDKs
  • Does not use any social media tracking
  • Does not transmit data to any external servers
  • Does not contain any in-app purchases

5. Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device for as long as the App is installed. We do not retain any user data on our servers because no data is ever collected or transmitted. When you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is automatically removed from your device.

6. Data Security

Since Mahjong Prime operates entirely offline and does not collect or transmit any data, there are no data security risks associated with data transmission. All game data remains securely within your device's local storage.

7. Children's Privacy

Mahjong Prime does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

8. Permissions

Mahjong Prime requests no special permissions on your device. The App does not require access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can always:

  • Clear locally stored game data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the App to remove all associated data
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions
